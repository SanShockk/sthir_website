Your 12-Week Journey

Three phases from Anchoring → Exploration → Rooting

Week 1

Title

    Phase 1 · Anchoring

    Notice patterns, slow down, and choose your focus.

    • Autopilot audit & attention science
    • Create your personal Anchoring Map
    • Commit a problem space to explore

    Phase 2 · Exploration

    Run small tests, collaborate, and learn from people.

    • Bold real-world experiments

    • Jobs-to-be-Done interviews & synthesis

    Phase 3 · Rooting

    Shape a point of view and ship a tangible outcome.

    • Influence & network building
    • MVP sprint with feedback loops
    • Leadership commitment & next steps

    The Meaning of Sthir (स्थिर)

    Sthir is स्थिर in Sanskrit

    In Sanskrit, 'Sthir' means stable, calm, and constant. It's the foundation upon which great things are built. Before you can build a successful company, you must first build a resilient self. We help you find your center, your 'Sthir', so you can stand tall and lead with confidence.
